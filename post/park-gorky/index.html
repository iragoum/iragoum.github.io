Park Gorky

Hello 👋, it’s me again Abderrahim and in this post i’m going to share with how i passed my last week in Park Gorky

Mougari Abderrahim
May 11, 2023 2 min read Demo
Image credit: By Abderrahim Mougari

After finishing my classes at the University of RUDN, I felt the urge to explore a bit more of Moscow’s beauty. So, I decided to take the metro to Gorky Park and enjoy a walk through this iconic landmark.

As I exited the metro station, the first thing that caught my attention was the stunning lighting in the park. It was like the whole place was covered with a warm blanket of light, creating a dreamy atmosphere that felt magical. I couldn’t wait to begin my exploration.

I started my walk by following the wide, paved path that led deeper into the park. The first thing that I saw was a large pond with a stunning fountain in the center. The water sparkled under the golden lights, and I could hear the sound of the water trickling from the fountain. The view was so soothing that I found myself taking deep breaths of the fresh, cool air.

As I continued my walk, I found myself surrounded by towering trees and lush greenery. The path wound its way through the park, taking me past tranquil ponds, ornate fountains, and carefully tended gardens. The sound of my footsteps on the path and the rustling of leaves underfoot was the only sound that broke the stillness of the night. It was like the whole park belonged to me.

As I walked, I came across a group of people gathered around a small stage. A band was playing lively music, and the crowd was swaying and dancing along. The energy was infectious, and I found myself smiling and tapping my foot along with the beat. I stayed for a while, enjoying the music and the atmosphere before continuing my walk.

As the night wore on, the park began to empty out, and the once-bustling paths grew quiet. The only sound was the distant hum of traffic and the occasional rustle of leaves in the wind. I took my time walking back to the metro station, savoring every moment of this peaceful and beautiful place.

As I left the park and headed back to the metro station, I couldn’t help but feel grateful for the chance to experience such a serene and stunning place. Walking through Gorky Park at night was an unforgettable experience that I would cherish forever.
